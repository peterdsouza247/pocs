The Philosophy

what these prototypes are proving
Stories over stats Players remember the ranger who never came home, not the +2 sword. Our systems generate moments worth retelling, then get out of the way.
Depth without weight Each prototype runs in a single file on a phone. The design carries the depth so the technology doesn't have to. Full productions scale the craft, not the complexity.
Real decisions Judgment calls with incomplete information: who to trust, what to tell them, which art to master. If a choice can be solved by a spreadsheet, it isn't a choice.

For Investors

Everything on this page was built to answer one question: is the core loop fun before a single dollar goes to production?

These prototypes validate the mechanics, the tone, and the audience at near-zero cost. The next step is full productions: art, sound, persistence, multiplayer, and platforms, built on loops that have already proven they hold attention.

Play them. If you lose track of time, that's the pitch.
