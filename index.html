The Philosophy

one rule across everything on this page
Stories over stats Players remember the ranger who never came home, not the +2 sword. Readers remember the dispatcher, not the body count. Systems and sentences alike should produce moments worth retelling, then get out of the way.
Language over numbers The games hide percentages behind an advisor's voice. The apps translate battery telemetry into "about 3 hours of writing." The books do it for a living. Numbers describe machines; language describes your day.
Depth without weight Every game here runs in a single file on a phone. The transfer site rebuilds itself from one data file. The desktop apps orchestrate what Linux already ships. The craft carries the depth so the technology doesn't have to.
